Providing for Those in Need

Vosloorus Children Home is non-profit organization that has been providing shelter for children that do not have a place called home for over a decade. The Vosloorus Children Home is located in the township of Vosloorus


Main Focus of The Foundation

The focus of the organization is to identify children from abused homes children living in the streets and children who can't care for the child
