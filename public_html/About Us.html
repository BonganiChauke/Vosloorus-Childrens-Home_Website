History

The Vosloorus Children's Home started in the year 2006 by Thandi Msibi, Mbali Kheswa and Palesa Motaung together touched by their tough childhood bringing moving from one foster to another having to worry about a place to sleep whether by the end of day the will have something on their stomach.

Mission

Our mission is to provide shelter to homeless, abused children and also to educate these young bright minds. by Thandi Msibi on 16 April 2006 the day the foundation was official open

Not only the main mission is to provide shelter but also to build strong mindful characters in these children teach them about the society we live in and protect them depression and substance abuse.


Vision

Just like an other organization our aim is to grow and multiply into other surrounding townships work with local schools and social worker's organization to identify children from hard backgrounds and to help children to be confident and aspire to be great in life.
